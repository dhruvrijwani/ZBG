Key Insights & Recommendations

Month-on-Month Performance Trends

Strong and consistent growth was observed from September to November, with ad spend scaling nearly 8× during this period. Revenue scaled efficiently alongside spend, peaking in November with the highest revenue of ₹8,28,306 and the strongest ROAS at 2.95.
Geographic Performance Insights

The India market emerged as the primary revenue driver, contributing approximately 84% of total purchases and revenue, while maintaining a strong ROAS of 2.41, above the overall account average.

The USA market demonstrated moderate efficiency with a ROAS of 2.19, though purchase volumes remained limited.

The UK market underperformed, recording the lowest ROAS of 0.85, with low conversion volume despite relatively consistent spend.
Overall Account Health

An overall ROAS of 2.35 indicates a profitable and scalable ad account. The total purchase volume of 166 aligns well with the current spend levels, though performance remains highly dependent on the India market.
Product-Level Performance Insights

Consistent performance was driven by Moissanite and Bridal Jewelry categories. Heavy, occasion-focused designs such as Necklace Sets, Chokers, Bridal Sets, and Kadas performed particularly well, indicating strong buyer intent for wedding and festive jewelry.
