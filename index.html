Key Insights & Recommendations

Sale periods continue to drive the majority of conversions and achieve higher ROAS.
US targeting shows strong potential, early data highlights high-value orders and good engagement.
Top products such as Zal Kundan Choker and Nivisha Bridal Set should be prioritized in future campaigns for consistent revenue contribution.
Creative direction: Catalog-style creatives and videos perform best, delivering stronger engagement and higher conversion rates.
Operational update: Shiprocket integration has been successfully implemented, resulting in a smoother checkout process. Users now experience auto-filled address fields, reducing drop-offs and improving purchase completion rates.

Next Steps

Gradually scale India targeting to ₹10,000/day to strengthen local market momentum.

Expand testing into new regions, starting with the UK, to diversify global audience reach.

Experiment with new audiences and creatives to continuously refine performance and optimize return on ad spend.
